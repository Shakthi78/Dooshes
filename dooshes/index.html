mooshes

Meet Mooshes - the stick figure that roasts everyone online. He's got quick wit, snappy comebacks, and a personality as spicy as a jalapeno. But this isn't just any art project - it's a social commentary on the wild world of online humor.

Welcome

From playful banter to downright offensive insults, Mooshes takes us on a journey through the highs and lows of internet culture. So, grab some popcorn and get ready to LOL or cringe - it's about to get real in here.

Lore

At first glance, a Moosh is a Moosh. Nothing more, nothing less. However, beyond the surface, Mooshes have a whole array of emotions they deal with.

From spurts of joy, to anger, to love, and fear-- Mooshes exhibit a vast range of emotions, often disregarded due to the outer attitude they exhibit towards the world.

With this in mind, we want to bring the complexity of the Moosh to the forefront of the academic conversation. Not only to showcase, but to exhibit, the multi faceted, complex being, which is the Moosh.

Whether you banged your buddies mom, bullied that one kid, leaked your sisters nudes, or ran over the neighborhood grandma's mailbox in your PT Cruiser, we're here for you.
